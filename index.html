The Contribution Pathway

Our unique mutual bereavement scheme operates with transparency, rapid mobilization, and deep diaspora commitment inside the UK.

01

Become a Member

Register yourself and dependents through an annual flat contribution fee (£10 per adult, £5 per child). Keep your membership current on our database.

Read eligibility specifications
02

Stand in Readiness

Maintain an active membership for 6 months. Once a bereavement within active registry ranks occurs, members are notified instantly to contribute £30 each.

View operational timelines
03

Support Mobilization

Upon a verified bereavement, our joint chest triggers an immediate £10,000 disbursement to the family. This ensures immediate repatriation arrangements.
