Who We Are & Why We Exist

Twende Zambia UK is a noble community mutual benefit society committed exclusively to raising practical structural support during mourning and repatriation emergencies inside the UK.

Why This Organisation Exists

For Zambian families residing in the United Kingdom, encountering sudden deaths / terminal illnesses within household lines represents immense psychological pain. Over and above grieving, families frequently construct enormous debt burdens trying to finance the expensive legal paperwork, preparation, flights, and safe cultural repatriation of their departed loved ones back home to Zambia.

Through the collaborative, unified strength of Twende Zambia UK, we eliminate this isolation. We bind together so that no individual family stands alone or goes into deep debt during severe times of mourning. Together, we manage immediate, high-volume financial security with absolute trust.

Our Diaspora Pledge

"During times of mourning, many individuals in our community encounter the challenging task of bringing their loved ones back home due to financial limitations." Through small, shared, highly secure £30 peer-to-peer contributions once a loss occurs, our goal is to deliver an instant £10,000 disbursement.

Our Core Mission

To support our fellow Zambians in the UK during times of mourning by establishing a highly transparent, rapid-trigger contribution system that raises up to £10,000 for families undergoing grief, protecting them against economic distress.

Our Visions for Sustainability

To see every Zambian diaspora family in the UK fully covered and protected under our sanctuary of mutual care. We envision a sustainable framework where unity replaces despair, and where repatriation is managed honorably without loan debt stress.

Sovereignty Disclosure

Strict Non-Profit & Non-Partisan Declaration

Let it be known to all members and external agencies that Twende Zambia UK is a purely humanitarian, mutual aid diaspora scheme. We exist independently of any government offices, state political parties, religious sects, or tribal affiliations.

We focus 100% of our energy, resources, and shared databases solely on coordinate welfare, bereavement support, and member security. Every single penny collected is held securely in trust, subjected regularly to rigorous independent audits, and returned straight to affected families with complete fiduciary integrity.

Neutral Platform Strictly non-factional welfare. No political campaigns permitted.
Fiduciary Transparency Regular financial audits shared directly with registered members in writing.
How we walk

Our Guiding Community Values

These elements frame how we communicate, handle decisions, manage registered collections, and honor our deceased families.

Integrity & Accountability

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standard. Financial reports, database entries, and transaction records are managed openly and subjected to regular reviews.

Mutual Solidarity

We operate in deep unison. By sharing the economic weight of bereavement trials together, we drastically reduce distress, giving every home standard stability.

Dignity for All

Every deceased member is treated with maximum cultural and spiritual honor. We ensure flight configurations, transport details, and casket preparations are first-rate.
